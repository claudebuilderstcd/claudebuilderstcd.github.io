We're excited to officially launch the TCD Claude Builder Club! Our mission is to bring together Trinity College Dublin students who are passionate about building with AI, specifically with Anthropic's Claude.

## What We Offer

- **Workshops** - Regular hands-on sessions to learn Claude API
- **Hackathons** - Opportunities to build and showcase projects
- **API Credits** - Free Claude Pro and API credits for members
- **Community** - Connect with like-minded builders

## How to Join

1. Fill out our [Interest Form](https://www.jotform.com/253555944387168)
2. Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/rFe8tJ88ww) server
3. Attend an event and fill out the Check-in Form

We can't wait to see what you'll build with Claude!
